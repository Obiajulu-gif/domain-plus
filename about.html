OUR SERVICES

Practical ERP services from planning to support.

We help businesses clarify requirements, deploy the right systems, support users, and build the skills needed to keep operations running confidently.

Consulting

We discuss our client's business requirements and interpret the facts, identify the challenges, and express the critical success factors for the required solution. Through our consulting services, we bring strategic thinking and actionable solutions to your business, focusing on both project-based and long-term goals.

We conduct thorough needs analysis and manage every phase, including development, implementation, training, and post-implementation support. We also design customized reporting and dashboards for management and executive management based on key performance areas in the business.

Implementation

We provide organizations with easy-to-use business management software and services, including enterprise resource planning, accounting packages, human resource payroll systems, employee self-service, customer relationship management, and payment processing.

Sage solutions help clients manage complex processes with confidence. Key features include multi-currency capability, bank reconciliation, tax reporting, payroll, HR insurance schemes, employee and management self-service, e-recruitment, flexible transaction processing, fiscal period management, and dashboards for analytical reporting.

Maintenance and Support

We offer outstanding customer service and support. Our customer-centric approach is designed to build customer loyalty while setting us apart from our competitors.

Our support and maintenance model is flexible and agreed with each client during implementation. From choosing the right solution to seamless integration and maintenance, we provide ongoing support throughout the product life cycle, backed by internationally recognized Sage Partner support.

Training

As part of software implementation, training sessions are organized at the client site for users of deployed business solutions. ICT, business process, and related training programmes are organized externally at the company's training center or in-house at client locations.

We provide comprehensive training to end users and system administrators so they can use the solution efficiently. Our training is delivered by certified and experienced specialists who bring practical experience and address real-world business challenges.
