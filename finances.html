FINANCIAL SERVICES

Ensuring Compliance for a Fast-Growing FinTech

The Challenge

As the FinTech company's user base and daily transaction volumes skyrocketed, their legacy accounting software began to buckle under the weight. End-of-month reconciliations took up to five days to complete, and maintaining strict regulatory compliance trails required grueling, error-prone manual data exports.

The Domain-Plus Solution

We engineered a high-performance, custom-integrated ERP environment capable of processing millions of micro-transactions seamlessly. We automated their general ledger entries and built strict, unalterable audit trails directly into the architecture to track every cent moving through the system.

The Impact

The finance team was transformed from an administrative bottleneck into a strategic asset. The automated compliance reporting easily satisfied regulatory audits without manual intervention, and the executive team gained a real-time, second-by-second view of the company's cash flow.
