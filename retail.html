RETAIL & eCOMMERCE

Driving Growth for a Regional Retail Chain

The Challenge

The retailer was struggling to balance their rapidly growing online store with their brick-and-mortar locations. Their physical Point of Sale (POS) systems were completely disconnected from their eCommerce platform. This meant online customers were constantly buying out-of-stock items, and store managers were spending hours every night manually reconciling online and physical sales data.

The Domain-Plus Solution

We delivered a true omnichannel ERP integration. We securely connected their backend inventory database simultaneously to their physical cash registers and their online storefront. Every time an item is scanned in-store or purchased online, the master stock level updates instantly across all platforms.

The Impact

The unified system completely eliminated "out-of-stock" online refunds, saving their brand reputation and customer trust. Furthermore, by automating the daily sales reconciliation, store managers were able to focus entirely on customer experience and floor strategy, directly contributing to a massive boost in year-over-year revenue.
