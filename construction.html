CONSTRUCTION & REAL ESTATE

Unifying Project Costs for a National Developer

The Challenge

The client's massive construction projects were plagued by unpredictable budget overruns. Material purchasing, contractor payroll, and head-office accounting were all handled in completely disconnected systems. Site managers lacked real-time visibility into actual costs versus budgets, often finding out they were over budget weeks after the fact.

The Domain-Plus Solution

We implemented a centralized, cloud-based ERP with customized modules specifically for construction management. We integrated their back-office accounting software directly with mobile, on-site procurement tools, allowing foremen to instantly log material costs and labor hours from the field.

The Impact

The developer gained a unified, live dashboard for all active sites. By catching budget leaks in real-time and streamlining the approval process for new materials, they prevented costly delays and drastically improved their overall profit margins per build.
